RNA phenotyping and genetic analysis portal

Explore Pantry and LaDDR results across human tissues.

This portal contains multimodal RNA phenotypes, covariates, cis-xQTL results from tensorQTL, and xTWAS weights and associations generated from Geuvadis and 49 GTEx tissues.

50 human tissue datasets

Knowledge-driven RNA phenotypes from Pantry are supplemented with residual data-driven phenotypes produced by LaDDR, plus full data-driven phenotypes that can be used as an alternative to knowledge-driven RNA phenotypes. The xTWAS data covers 114 traits using GWAS data from this collection.
